Quality Assurance (QA) services for any health care software, we will be responsible for creating and implementing a strategy for quality coordination and testing as well as suggesting solutions to identified quality problems. Few task involved are:

  1. Test current products and identifying deficiencies
  2. Suggest solutions to identified product problems
  3. Investigate product quality in order to make improvements to achieve better customer satisfaction
  4. Identify quality assurance process bottleneck and suggest actions for improvement
  5. Code automated tests
  6. Review user interfaces for consistency and functionality
